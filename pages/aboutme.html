Art First

We have an art first mentality. We try to create things that people can enjoy and be moved by. Our creativity and imaginiation are our values we hold most dear. It is because of this that we believe in the process, and will never use any sort of generative AI tools to replace the creative process.

Users Second

Mods are what keep our games alive. Our game engine and titles will always support modding and tinkering. It is because of this that we will never release our game with software that stops modification. Mod it till it breaks!

Profits Third

Money is something required to keep our studio running. By purchasing a game you are directly supporting us. However money can also cause problems in games. We believe that real world and in game items should be seprate. And that a fantasy stops being a fantasy when you need to pay for it in the real world. No microtransactions, no crypto, no NFTs, and NO predatory marketing practices.
